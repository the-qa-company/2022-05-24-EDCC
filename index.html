What is the aim of the EDCC KB?

  • Allow the European Commission to have access to the data of the EDCC contact center. This includes data about requests and responses, as well as to all resources used to answer the requests posed by the citizens.
  • Create an infrastructure to facilitate and speed up the work of the Communication Officers in their daily task of answering questions.

  3. Compute the sentiment
